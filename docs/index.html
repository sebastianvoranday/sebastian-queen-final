The British Band that Rocked the World

Formed in 1970, Queen took the world by storm. Led by iconic frontman Freddie Mercury, members Brian May, Roger Taylor and John Deacon participated as part of what many consider to be the only "real" lineup for the legendary rock band.

Within seven years, Queen cemented itself in history with timeless classics that have been used in every medium possible. Even for those youngsters who do not know who "Queen" is, they find themselves stomping their feet and clapping their hands to the unmistakable "We Will Rock You."

Their long lasting impact is even evident among Gen Z college students, with professors assigning final projects all about the band and its accomplishments. When completing these projects, students undoubtedly feel as though they are the champions.
